Frequently asked questions

Is Tokens really free?

Yes. Generating codes, unlimited accounts, Face ID lock, and local backup are free forever. Tokens Pro is optional and only unlocks extras like encrypted sync, Apple Watch, and the Mac app.

How do I import from Google Authenticator?

In Google Authenticator, choose “Transfer accounts” → “Export accounts” to display a QR code, then scan it with Tokens. All your accounts move over at once.

Are my codes safe if I lose my phone?

If you enabled iCloud Sync, your accounts are restored end-to-end encrypted on your new device. You can also export an encrypted backup file at any time.

Can you see my codes?

No. Your secrets are stored only on your devices and, if you enable sync, in your own iCloud with a password only you know. We have no servers that hold your data.
